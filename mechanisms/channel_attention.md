# Channel Attention

Channel Attention evaluates the inter-dependencies between feature map channels to emphasize meaningful object characteristics.

## 🖼️ Diagram
![Channel Attention Diagram](../images/channel_attention.png)

## 📄 Original Paper
- **Title:** [Squeeze-and-Excitation Networks](https://arxiv.org/abs/1709.01507)
- **Year:** 2017
- **Authors:** Hu et al.

## 💡 Key Concept
It focuses on "what" is important by recalibrating channel-wise feature responses, effectively modeling the importance of different feature detectors.
