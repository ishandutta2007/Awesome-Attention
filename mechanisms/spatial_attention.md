# Spatial Attention

Spatial Attention focuses on specific geometric regions within an image.

## 🖼️ Diagram
![Spatial Attention Diagram](../images/spatial_attention.png)

## 📄 Original Paper
- **Title:** [Spatial Transformer Networks](https://arxiv.org/abs/1506.02025)
- **Year:** 2015
- **Authors:** Jaderberg et al.

## 💡 Key Concept
It identifies "where" to look in an image by generating a spatial importance mask, helping the model ignore irrelevant background and focus on key objects.
