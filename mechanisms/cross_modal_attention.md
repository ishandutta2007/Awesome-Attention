# Cross-Modal Attention

Cross-Modal Attention aligns information across different types of media, such as mapping text tokens to visual patches.

## 🖼️ Diagram
![Cross-Modal Attention Diagram](../images/cross_modal.png)

## 📄 Original Paper
- **Title:** [Show, Attend and Tell: Neural Image Caption Generation with Visual Attention](https://arxiv.org/abs/1502.03044)
- **Year:** 2015
- **Authors:** Xu et al.

## 💡 Key Concept
It allows the model to "look" at specific regions of an image when generating or processing specific words, enabling deep multimodal understanding.
